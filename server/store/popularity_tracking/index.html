Popularity Tracking

When Popularity Tracking is turned on, Personas for Firefox will ping a Mozilla server each time you select a new Persona. We count the number of pings for each Persona design in order to determine its popularity, and to populate the Most Popular category.

No personally-identifiable information is transfered to Mozilla with this ping.

In addition to the name of the Persona selected, like most Web enabled services, Personas for Firefox does collect non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, and date and time of each visitor request. Mozilla also collects potentially-personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, which are non-personally-identifying in and of themselves but could be used in conjunction with other information to personally identify users.

Please consult the Mozilla Privacy Policy contains additional information on our data protection practices.
