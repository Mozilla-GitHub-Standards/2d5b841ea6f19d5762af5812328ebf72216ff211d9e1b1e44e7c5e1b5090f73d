Getting Started

Change the look and feel of your browser to match your mood!

  1. Click the little Fox mask in the lower-left corner of your Firefox.
  2. Select your first Persona from the list.
  3. Enjoy the artwork.

Please join us in the Mozilla Labs discussion forum to provide feedback or make suggestions.
