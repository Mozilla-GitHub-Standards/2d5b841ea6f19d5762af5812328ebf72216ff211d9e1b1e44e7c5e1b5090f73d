Get Started with Personas

  1. Click on the fox mask in the lower left corner of your Firefox browser, or go to the Persona’s page directly from here.
  2. Next, select a Persona from the list, or check out the Persona’s Gallery.
  3. You can change your Persona as much as you like! Choose a new one from the list or create your own.
